To watch EgressProfiles:
  kubectl get egressprofiles -A

To create your first profile:
  kubectl apply -f https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ihsenalaya/egress-guardian-operator/main/config/samples/egressprofile_observe.yaml

Observe → Suggest → Enforce workflow:
  1. Create an EgressProfile with mode: Observe
  2. Wait for baseline to build (check status.observedDestinationsCount)
  3. Switch to mode: Suggest — a policy proposal is generated
  4. Review the generated CiliumNetworkPolicy in the ConfigMap
  5. Approve: kubectl patch egresspolicyproposal <name> --type=merge -p '{"spec":{"approval":{"approved":true}}}'
  6. Switch to mode: Enforce — the CNP is applied

WARNING: Enforcing puts the workload under DEFAULT-DENY egress.
Watch for Events of type DefaultDenyEgress before approving.
